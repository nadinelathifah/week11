Welcome

Hi, my name is Nadine. I'm currently a postgraduate student at PPU and the Head of Foreign Exchange Soc, and I'll be taking you through this society's programmes and activities.

The PPU Foreign Exchange Soc is a dynamic society that offers a study-abroad programme at one of our partner universities. As part of the society, members can gain the chance to experience new cultures, learn new languages, explore different cuisines, and discover global industries. This programme allows students to forge lasting friendships and network with peers from diverse backgrounds.

The study abroad programme lasts a period of 3 months (1 semester) and runs every year during 2nd semester of every course academic year. At the end of each programme, students will get an opportunity to share their experiences at our annual seminar and attend a CV workshop.

Students from any degree programme or year can join, however, slots are limited so we reccommend signing up as soon as possible. If you are a returning student who enrolled in the society last year, you must enrol again to access the study-abroad programme for this year.
