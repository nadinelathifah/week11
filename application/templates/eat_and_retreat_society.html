Join Eat and Retreat Society!

If you love to try local delicacies and explore the hidden gems of the city, this is the place for you!




Eat & Retreat Society is a community of adventurous food lovers and explorers who seek out the best local delicacies while uncovering hidden gems in every area we visit. From charming hole-in-the-wall eateries to off-the-beaten-path cultural experiences, we believe that the best way to connect with a place is through its flavors and stories. Whether it’s savoring authentic street food, discovering family-run restaurants, or indulging in unique culinary experiences, we are on a mission to eat, explore, and retreat into the heart of local culture.

The Eat & Retreat Society meets regularly, typically once or twice a month, depending on the destination and event. We organize planned gatherings around food festivals, seasonal specialties, and unique local experiences. Sometimes, we arrange spontaneous meetups for pop-up restaurants, newly discovered hidden gems, or exclusive tasting events. Members can stay updated on upcoming meetups through our social media channels and group discussions.

Anyone with a love for food, adventure, and cultural exploration can join the Eat & Retreat Society! Whether you're a seasoned foodie, a casual diner, or simply someone who enjoys discovering new places and flavors, you're welcome to be part of our community. There are no strict requirements—just an open mind, a curious palate, and a willingness to explore. We welcome solo travelers, groups of friends, and even families who share our passion for uncovering hidden gems and indulging in delicious local delicacies.
